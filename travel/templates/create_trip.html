You are one step
to create your own trip.

In order to create trip you have to do following steps:

  • Choose type of trip. There are three options: multiple, single, family.
    1. Multiple: you will have option to travel in group. Maximum number of members might be 10. You can pick the guide and services from his list. As well as, join to the existing group.
    2. Single: you will travel alone.
    3. Family: you can travel with your family, choose number of people to travel. The system will create separate group for you. We have 10% discount for family groups.

    Pick hotel to stay.

    Choose type of meal. There are options: Guide will organize the food according to your desires.
    Choose dates.

    After submitting in the system will be created a trip for you, but to finish you have to choose in next pages guides and services.
